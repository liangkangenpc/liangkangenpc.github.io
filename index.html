About

I am a transportation and urban systems researcher specializing in home-to-work commuting, spatial interaction modeling, transport econometrics, and transport–environment coupling.

My work bridges gravity models, discrete choice models, and entropy maximization, with applications in policy simulation, job accessibility, and carbon emissions.
